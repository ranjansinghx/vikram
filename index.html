🔒 Privacy Policy
Your Data Stays on Your Device
Vikram stores all your data — events, notes, tasks, cycle records, and settings — exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. No account is required to use the app.
Google Sign-In (Optional)
If you choose to sign in with Google, Vikram requests read-only access to your Google Calendar so you can view those events alongside the Nepali calendar. Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Google's secure OAuth flow — Vikram never sees your password.
No Tracking, No Ads
Vikram does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking. There are no cookies beyond what your browser sets for localStorage.
Push Notifications
If you enable event reminders, your browser may request notification permission. Notification payloads are generated locally — no data leaves your device.
Deleting Your Data
You can delete your data at any time via Settings → Data & Privacy. "Reset All App Data" removes everything Vikram has stored locally. You can also clear notes, tasks, or events individually.
